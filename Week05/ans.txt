Based on the videos and the reading material, how would you define a data scientist and data science? (3 marks)

Data scientist is a professional who is responsible for collecting, analyzing, interpreting and transforming 
large volumes of data. Data scientist is a specialist in the field of data science. 
It is an interdisciplinary field that combines the sciences of statistics, mathematics, and computer science.
In a given organization, data scientists bridge the gap between the sciences and the business. Through collaboration, with the data scientist, the business can benefit from the insights gained from the data.
Data science includes machine learning, data visualization, data mining, data analysis and interpretation,
which provides the business with the necessary insights to make decisions and improve the performance of the business.
